About Me

hello, friends.

My name is Caleb Myers. I'm an aspiring software developer in the UNCC Coding Bootcamp. I went to college and studied English Literature, but toward the end of my program I became interested in programming. Ever since writing my first "hello, world" I've been hooked.

My studies in English improved my writing, analysis, and creative thinking. These skills combined with a love for technology and figuring out how things work have made programming a newfound passion of mine. I hope to take this time to learn, and eventually work on great projects.

Apart from programming, my main interests lie in music, movies, and video games. I love all sorts of Rock, Electronic, and Hip-hop music, while some of my favorites are Gorillaz, Incubus, deadmau5, MGMT, Pretty Lights and \ so much more. Some of my favorite movies are Dazed and Confused, Orange County, There Will Be Blood, and Pulp Fiction. Video games have been a huge part of my life ever since I first started playing on a Nintendo 64. Some all time favorites are Final Fantasy X, The Elder Scrolls IV: Oblivion, and World of Warcraft.
